Cataracts

Quick Info

  • Clouding of the lens of the eye
  • Usually related to aging
  • Hard time seeing at night
  • Blurry vision
  • Glary vision
  • Images appear dull / yellow
  • Get removal surgery only when your doctor reccomends it

What is Cataracts

Cataracts is the clouding of the lens in the eye which is usually clear

When this happens, it makes it hard for the light rays to go into the retina after getting refracted by the lens.

Cataracts usally happens when a protein called crystallin starts to break down and clump together

What are the causes of Cataracts?

Aging is the most common cause of Cataracts

Other causes include:

  • Diabetes
  • Eye injury
  • Eye Surgery
  • Radiation treatment on the upper body
  • Spending a lot of time in the sun without sunglasses
  • Steroids
  • Smoking / Drinking

How will Cataracts affect your life?

Having Cataracts will have a minor - huge impact on your life depending on how severe it is

  • It will make images appear to be duller
  • You will have glary vision
  • You will be more sensitive to light
  • You will have a harder time seeing at night
  • If it gets severe enough, it can lead to vision loss

What should I do if I get Cataracts

If you contract Cataracts, you should try to do the following things:

  • Stop smoking / drinking
  • Get frequent eye exams
  • Wear sunglasses when outside
  • Try not to drive at night
