Strabismus

Quick Info

  • Strabismus happens when muscles in the eye lose control over where the pupil is
  • Causes alignment issues
  • Can cause double vision, starin on the eye
  • Can be caused by head injury, neurological problems, brain tumors, cerebral palsy, hydrocephalus
  • Can be treated using surgery and contact lenses

What is Strabismus

Our eye has muscles that control the movement of our eye

When this happens, the eye can be drift off center causing it to look cross eyed

Different types of Strabismus(look at image) depending on how the eye drifts

Not harmful to vision but can be nusiance to deal with because of all the headaches and strain on the eyes

Causes of Strabismus

Can be caused for a multitude of reasons including:

  • Graves' disease
  • Head Injury
  • Down syndrome
  • Brain tumors
  • Stroke
  • Neurological issues
  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Hydrocephalus

How to treat it

There are 2 ways to treat it

Contact lenses that can coorect the refractive error in the eye because of Strabismus

The other is to get surgery to realign your eyes. This may not fix vision but can help with self confidence
