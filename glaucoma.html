Glaucoma

Quick Info

  • Build up of pressure in the eye which causes damage to the optical nerve of the eye
  • Caused by not being able to dry the aqueous fluid in the eye
  • Blurry vision
  • Blood shot eyes
  • Eye pain
  • Overtime vision loss
  • Glaucoma laser surgery can lead to Cataracts

What is Glaucoma

Glaucoma is the damage of the optical nerve caused by pressur in the eye

This pressure is caused by the buildup of aqueous fluid in the eye and not being drained out

When this happens, the optical nerve has a hard time converting light into electrical signal which tells the brain what to see and where

What are the causes of Glaucoma

Glaucoma is caused when the aqueous fluid in the eye doesn't drain out normally

This increases pressure in the eye and causes damage to the optical nerve

How will Glaucoma affect your life

Glaucoma will cause blind spots in your vision over time

In the final stages of Glaucoma, full vision loss will occur

Treatment for Glaucoma

Unfortunately there is no way to get rid of Glaucoma

The main goal of Glaucoma treatment is to stop the progression or slow down the progression of Glaucoma

There are 2 main types of surgeries that doctors do to treat Glaucoma

Laser Surgery:

  • Laser surgery is obviously done with the help of a laser
  • The eye is numbed with the help of eye drops and then a laser is used to drain out the extra fluid

Traditonal surgey is based off of incsisions that help drain the fluid out

THESE SURGERIES DO NOT REMOVE GLAUCOMA AND INSTEAD STOP IT OR SLOW IT DOWN. IT IS STILL IMPORTANT TO GET REGULAR CHECK UPS AFTER YOU GET THE SURGERY
