Astigmatism

Quick Info

  • Caused by abnormal shape of the cornea
  • Ligh doesnt refract as needed
  • Blurry vision
  • Headaches
  • Glasses or Contact lenses help
  • Surgery in extreme cases
  • Contact lenses can, in some cases, increase astigmatism
  • Make sure your prescription glasses are up to date with your vision needs

What is Astigmatism

Happens when the cornea is shaped in a weird way

Refracts light in a weird way

Blurry visions and headaches are commons

Causes of astigmatism

Doctors don't know why the cornea changes takes a weird shape

How will astigmatism affect your life

Because of astigmatism, you will have frequent headaches

Blurr vision will also be common

Treatment for Astigmatism

For mild cases of Astigmatism, glasses / Contact lenses should do the trick in avoiding its negative affects

In worst case scenarios, Doctors reccomend you to have surgery to treat it
