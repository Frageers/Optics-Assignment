Macular Degeneration

Quick Info

  • Degeneration of the macula in the retina which is responsible for the central vision
  • Cannot be removed but can be slowed down
  • Hard time doing every day tasks as it progresses
  • No treatment for dry macular degeneraton
  • No way to find it in the early stages
  • Even after surgery may need it to get another surgery

What is Macular Degeneration

Degeneration of the Macula in the eye which is responsible for the central vision

When this happens, the optical nerve isnt getting the light from the retina for the central part of your vision

That light isnt converted into the electrical signal which is how we see

What are the causes?

Usually related to age

Higher risk if you are caucasian, have diabetes

Can also be caused by a head injury or if your diet doesnt have sufficient nutrients

How will it affect your daily life

You will start to see straight lines as curved

If not treated, you will lose your central vision

Until then, it will be harder and harder to do everyday tasks like cooking and cleaning

Will also create blind spots in your vision

Treatments for Macular Degeneration

There are only treatmentrs that exist for wet Macular Degeneration

One possible treatment is to use lasers

The doctor will numb your eyes and put in a special contact lens that helps guide the laser to the abonromal blood clots and remove them / block them


The second option is to use VGEF medicine

The doctor will numb your eyes then clean them

The medicine will be inserted through a small needle
